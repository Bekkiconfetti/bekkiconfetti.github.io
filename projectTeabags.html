Teabags

It started in the first semester, when used teabags were starting to accumulate on my desk as a by-product from my work at the studio.

I was fascinated by their color scheme and diverse shapes, so I started collecting and mounting them on the wall behind me.

During the first exhibition, I mounted them on a flat surface, showcasing the richness of the resulting texture and play of colors. In the next step I explored their potential in a more dynamic setting and staged them on the human body.
