Who's your Dado?

It's not a coincidence that you might feel like you don't know much about your dado. It is the surveillance capitalist business model to use your dado to find out more about your future user behavior without your knowledge or consent. To disguise this practice, companies use their position as mediators to limit communication between humans and dados to formats in line with their agendas, leaving many parts unknown and unaccessible to humans.
