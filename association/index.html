Meet your Dado

Your Dado, or data double, is made up of all digital information referenced to you as an individual. Dados are becoming significant players because it's their role to represent humans in the expanding digital realm. At this very moment, yours is probably interacting with several businesses, public authorities, and even potential lovers on your behalf.

And yet, how much do you know about what your dado is doing? Our mission at the Association for Human & Dado Friendship is to get you more in touch with your digital presence and to help you make sure your dado is representing your best interests.
