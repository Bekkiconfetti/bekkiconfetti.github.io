OFFICE FOR ELECTRONIC ENCOUNTERS

At the office for electronic encounters, I build embodied interfaces that mediate between material and digital realities. These can take the form of interactive installations, wearables, experimental controllers for games and generative visuals, with a special love for projects that combine textiles & electronics.

"The Heart of the Hurt" by Teresa Fernandez Pello
Shown at the Dordrecht Museum as part of "A Room with a View" from 25.06.2023 - 14.01.2024
Image and technical drawing Credits: Teresa Fernández Pello
Technical Set-Up: Multidisplay installation featuring LCD Screens showing videos and LED Dot Matrix Displays showing generated horoscope sentences.
Scope: Prototyping of electronics and software, featuring a custom python script to generate horoscope sentences using Teresa's Google search history and display them on the LED Matrices in real-time.
Development of exhibition proof, plug & play system for all displays.
