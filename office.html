Office for Electronic Encounters

At the Office for Electronic Encounters, I work together with other creatives to realize their ideas with electronics and code. I have built interactive installations, custom portable controllers, and wearable sensors, I've programmed generative visuals, built web applications, and many other things. My special love is for projects that combine textiles with electronics.
Find some examples of previous collaborations below.

Teresa Fernandez Pello

2024

"Comp. Core Sacra" by Teresa Fernandez Pello
Shown at Galerie Paris Horizon as part of "Beautiful Stranger" from 22.03.2024-21.04.2024
Image Credit: Teresa Fernández Pello

Technical Set-Up: Installation featuring multiple LED Dot Matrix Displays, partly showing live generated sentences describing ai, using keywords from a database of AI-related articles
Scope: Prototyping of electronics and software, featuring a custom python script to generate sentences describing ai, using keywords from a database of ai-related articles and display them on the LED Matrices in real-time. Development of exhibition proof, plug & play system for all displays.

Teresa Fernandez Pello

"The Heart of the Hurt" by Teresa Fernandez Pello
Shown at the Dordrecht Museum as part of "A Room with a View" from 25.06.2023 - 14.01.2024
Image and technical drawing Credits: Teresa Fernández Pello

Technical Set-Up: Multidisplay installation featuring LCD Screens showing videos and LED Dot Matrix Displays showing generated horoscope sentences.
Scope: Prototyping of electronics and software, featuring a custom python script to generate horoscope sentences using Teresa's Google search history and display them on the LED Matrices in real-time.
Development of exhibition proof, plug & play system for all displays.
